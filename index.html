About ToolKit

ToolKit was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, easy-to-use file conversion and optimization tools that are completely free and accessible to everyone.

Our team of developers and designers are passionate about creating tools that solve real problems for our users. We believe that everyone should have access to powerful file processing tools without having to pay expensive subscription fees or install complicated software.

Our Values

  • Simplicity: We believe tools should be intuitive and easy to use
  • Privacy: Your files never leave your computer - we process everything locally
  • Quality: We're committed to providing the best possible results
  • Accessibility: Our tools are free and work on any device with a web browser

Today, ToolKit serves millions of users worldwide and continues to expand its offerings with new tools and features regularly.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2023

At ToolKit, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our website and tools.

Information We Don't Collect

The most important thing to know about ToolKit is that we process all files locally in your browser. This means:

  • Your files never leave your computer
  • We never upload your files to our servers
  • We don't have access to your files
  • We don't store your files

Information We Do Collect

We collect minimal information to help us improve our services:

  • Usage Analytics: We use anonymous analytics to understand how people use our tools so we can improve them
  • Error Reports: If a tool crashes, we may receive an anonymous error report to help us fix the issue
  • Basic Device Information: We collect information about your browser and device to ensure our tools work correctly

Cookies

We use a minimal number of cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Provide analytics about how our tools are used
  • Ensure the proper functioning of our website

Third-Party Services

We use a few trusted third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: To understand how our website is used
  • Cloudflare: For content delivery and security

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@toolkit.com.
